About

Dr. Hong-Kyun Bae is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Kookmin University. He leads the PRecS Lab (Personalized Recommender Systems Lab), where his research focuses on artificial intelligence, recommender systems, information retrieval, and learning-to-rank.

Research Interests

  • Recommender Systems and Information Retrieval
  • Model Efficiency and Lightweight Architectures
  • Multi-objective Optimization in Ranking
  • Learning-to-Rank (LTR) Algorithms

About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Kookmin University. I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Inha University. In 2023, I served as a visiting scholar at Pennsylvania State University. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Hanyang University in 2024, supervised by Professor Sang-Wook Kim. My research areas include Recommender Systems, Learning-to-Rank, and Multi-Objective Optimization.

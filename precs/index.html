PRecS Lab

Personalized Recommender Systems Lab @ Kookmin University

About the Lab

We specialize in creating AI-driven solutions to address major challenges in Recommender Systems and Information Retrieval.
Our primary research areas include enhancing recommendation accuracy, increasing model efficiency, and supporting multi-objective optimization.

Research Topics

Accuracy & Multi-Objective Optimization

Improving recommendation quality while balancing accuracy, fairness, novelty, and diversity.

Learning-to-Rank (LTR)

Refining user behavior modeling with advanced ranking loss and sequential context awareness.

Model Lightweighting

Enabling real-time personalized recommendations with compact and efficient architectures.

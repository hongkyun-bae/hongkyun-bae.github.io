About the Lab

The PRecS Lab specializes in creating AI-driven solutions to address major challenges in Recommender Systems and Information Retrieval. Our primary research areas include enhancing recommendation accuracy, increasing model efficiency, and supporting multi-objective optimization.

Research Topics

  • Enhancing recommendation accuracy and multi-objective optimization
  • Refining Learning-to-Rank (LTR) techniques for user behavior modeling
  • Model lightweighting and real-time recommendation

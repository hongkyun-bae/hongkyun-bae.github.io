Personalized Recommender Systems Lab @ Kookmin University

About the Lab

The PRecS Lab focuses on developing AI-based approaches for key challenges in recommender systems and information retrieval. Our main research themes include improving recommendation accuracy, model efficiency, and multi-objective optimization.

Research Topics

  • Enhancing recommendation accuracy and multi-objective optimization
  • Learning-to-Rank (LTR) and user behavior modeling
  • Model lightweighting and real-time recommendation

PRecS Lab

Personalized Recommender Systems Lab @ Kookmin University

About the Lab

We focus on developing AI-driven solutions to solve key challenges in Recommender Systems and Information Retrieval.
Our main research areas include improving recommendation accuracy, boosting model efficiency, and supporting multi-objective optimization.

We are seeking self-motivated and enthusiastic graduate students and undergraduate research interns.
If you're interested in joining our team or have any questions, don't hesitate to get in touch with us at hongkyun{@}kookmin.ac.kr with a brief introduction of yourself, along with your CV and transcript.

Key Achievements

News Recommender Systems

Refining user behavior modeling with advanced ranking losses (presented at AAAI 2023).

POI Recommender Systems

Improving a pair-wise LTR technique by considering the domain-specific characteristics (presented at WWW 2024).

Multi-Objective Optimization

Proposing a list-wise LTR method for multi-objective optimization (presented at WWW 2025).

Broadcasting Show Recommender Systems

Enabling user modeling with a competition-aware characteristic of the broadcasting system (presented at ICDE 2023).

Profie-aware Recommender Systems

Boosting the rankings of profitable items to maximize the platform's profits (presented at WWW 2024 Poster).
